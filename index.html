    Taarangana

    Sparking Dreams, Amplifying Realities

    Welcome to the heartbeat of Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women, Taarangana, which represents the spirit of creativity, innovation, and sheer exuberance. It was founded to provide a forum for zealous youngsters to demonstrate their artistic abilities and has developed into a signature event that transcends boundaries. Over the years, we have witnessed an incredible voyage of advancement that has raised the bar time and time again in terms of scale, participation, and impact!


    The main aim of Taarangana has always been to bring out the best talents and showcase them to the world, and we are more prepared than ever before! We extend a warm welcome to everyone. Come along as we apply talent, passion, and imagination to the canvas of our yearly spectacle.
