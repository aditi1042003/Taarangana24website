VIKSIT BHARAT @2047

At the forefront of change and progress, Viksit Bharat stands as a guiding force, driving development across different parts of our nation. Serving as a catalyst for growth within our borders, Viksit Bharat takes a comprehensive approach to foster progress.

The mission of Viksit Bharat resonates with the idea of a cultural revival, aiming to blend timeless values with modern aspirations. This dynamic movement recognizes the importance of including everyone in development, working to spread progress to every part of our diverse country. In line with the G20's commitment to global financial stability and sustainable development, Viksit Bharat ambitiously seeks to build a united and flourishing society.

Taarangana'24 embraces the fundamental values of Viksit Bharat, aiming to foster innovation, education, and socio-economic well-being. This cultural extravaganza acts as a platform to share the principles of Viksit Bharat, creating an environment where cultural expression seamlessly intertwines with progress. Through these efforts, we aim to instill a deep sense of pride in our cultural heritage, steering our nation towards a brighter and more developed future. “Viksit Bharat – the crossroads where culture converges with progress, and traditions pave the way for a flourishing tomorrow.”
