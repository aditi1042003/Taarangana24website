Ms. and Mr. Taarangana

As we unwrap this epic splendor, brace yourself for the ultimate glamour showdown at Taarangana'24's esteemed title pageant, Mr. and Ms. Taarangana! Where charisma encapsulates authenticity, style, and wits. It’s a celebration of individuality and flair as participants contend to seize the crown. This allure of extraordinary sophistication promises to sweep you off of your feet. Jump aboard and join the fancy adventure.

ALAAP

Embrace the spotlight and let your melody soar at Alaap, Taarangana's premier singing contest! From soulful ballads to electrifying performances, this is your stage to showcase the depth of your musical prowess.
Whether you're a seasoned vocalist or a hidden gem waiting to be discovered, this is your moment to shine!

RANGMANCH

Welcome to the theatrical wonderland of Rangmanch at Taarangana’24 – where solo actors become storytellers, weaving tales that come alive on stage! It's a rollercoaster of laughter, tears, and jaw-dropping moments.
Calling all solo performers to take center stage and captivate hearts with your monologues, mimicry, and pure acting prowess. Rangmanch is not just a competition; it's a journey of bringing characters to life, creating moments that linger in the audience's minds. So, rehearse those lines, perfect your expressions, and get ready to steal the show!

AAGHAZ

Lights, Camera, Drama!
Aghaaz, the nukkad-natak competition is the stage for all performances to captivate the audience with your charismatic performances and enchanting storytelling. Calling all the performers who want to make their voice heard and bring their story to life! Bring the fire and passion to the street that awaits you.

JASHN E ALFAAZ

Welcome to Jashn-e-Alfaaz, where language dances in the symphony of emotions. Prepare to be enchanted by the eloquence of verse and the power of prose. In this celebration of words, every syllable is a brushstroke on the canvas of your imagination, Every stanza has a melody that resonates with your soul.
Join us as we honor the art of language and embrace the magic of expression. Indulge in the boundless realms of creativity and immerse yourself in the endless possibilities that await within the pages of Jashn-e-Alfaaz.

ANTRA

When the notes of melody entwine with the lyrical harmony, molten gold pours out of hearts with the beautiful vocals of singers to take the world on their toes as rhythm breaks into the emotions and energy of each and every soul.

LILAC DREAMS

“Never go out of style”
Lilac Dreams is the perfect fusion of fashion, creativity and storytelling. Weave your thoughts into a captivating tale and adorn your best attire. Flaunt your style as it unleashes your imagination and the journey to the title. Gear up for the ultimate battle as each team represents a new thread of artistry as you compete in the fashion world. Let your vision shine through in the characters you embody. The world of fashion and glamour awaits your presence! Taarangana’24 welcomes fashionistas to LILAC DREAMS: The world of fashion unveils

URBAN THUMP

The ultimate dance showdown is back, where your steps speak louder than words. From bhangra to bang, from classical to contemporary, whether you’re a classical artist, a hip-hop star, or a street dancer, this is your chance to be seen, heard, and remembered. The stage is set to be your canvas, let your creativity flow and your passion ignite the city streets. Join us in celebrating the diverse tapestry of dance, where every step tells a story, and every dancer is a storyteller. Urban Thump is much more than a group dance competition; it's a community, a vibrant ensemble of people who share a common language – the language of rhythm.
Urban Thump – Because your dance journey deserves a stage as bold and dynamic as you are!

RAP BATTLE

Are melodic rhymes with electrifying flows your genre of choice?
At Rap Battle, prepare to be blown away by jaw-dropping performances by the next rap Gods. The showtime for which you have been waiting for so long is finally here!

Equip your stage persona with your original and impactful rap compositions and establish yourself as the next rap genius!
